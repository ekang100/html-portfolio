About Me

I am a student at Duke University studying Computer Science and Statistics. I am interested in applying my skills in SWE, PM, and other technical roles where I can problem-solve, learn, and make things happen.

Outside of my academics and student orgs, I enjoy reading, exploring restaurants, and drinking coffee in my quest to find the best iced mocha latte. Currently, my favorite is from Beyu on campus.

I'm from Williamsburg, Virginia - a small, historic town you probably visited for your 6th-grade field trip. Born in North Carolina, however, returning to Duke has been a full-circle experience, and I call Durham home as well. In the future, I look forward to exploring new places as I immerse myself in new experiences.
